## BALANCE NOTES

**Guns are intentionally rare:**
- Level 4: Flintlocks = first guns
- Level 5: Modern firearms = unicorn rare
- Ammo crafting is HARD
- Save bullets for emergencies

**This creates gameplay:**
- Early game: Melee only
- Mid game: Bows, crossbows
- Late game: Flintlocks (rare)
- End game: Modern guns (unlikely)

**Carlos Garcia CAN'T make an AK-47:**
- He can fix engines
- He can make mechanical things
- He CAN'T manufacture firearms
- Realistic!
