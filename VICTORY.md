# OUTPOST ZERO - Victory Conditions & Curing System

## VICTORY OVERVIEW

### The Goal

**Win Condition:** Clear ALL alien plants (Miasma) from the map

**Steps to Win:**
1. Reach Tech Level 5
2. Research "Miasma Herbicide" (requires Laboratory)
3. Build "Recovery Medical Bay" (Tech 4+)
4. Cure infected survivors/animals
5. Send teams to clear Miasma zones
6. Eliminate all alien plants
7. Victory! Choose to:
   - Stay and build community
   - Move to new map (randomly generated)

## THE SCIENCE: PARASITE BIOLOGY

### How the Alien Plants Work

**Parasite Lifecycle:**

```
1. SPORES land on skin
   ↓
2. ROOTS grow into body (hours)
   ↓
3. TENDRILS reach brain (days)
   ↓
4. CONTROL ESTABLISHED (1-2 weeks)
   ↓
5. HOST becomes "infected" (zombie/mutation)
   ↓
6. PLANT grows from host (if in Miasma)
```

### How Parasites Control Hosts

**The Science (in-game):**
- Tendrils connect to nervous system
- **Block brain signals** to limbs
- **Send own signals** to move host
- **Interpret sensory input** (eyes, ears, skin)
- Host can still see/hear but can't control body
- Parasite uses host for: attacks, spreading, building

### Implications

**Why you can't just kill zombies:**
- Dead hosts in Miasma become new plants
- Kill one = new growth
- Must cure BEFORE killing

**Why fast death is bad:**
- Parasite dies quickly in host
- Releases ALL toxins at once
- Toxic shock kills host
- Anyone nearby also poisoned

## THE CURING PROCESS

### Finding Infected

**Infected = Not fully turned yet**
- Early stage (first 2 weeks): Can be saved!
- Late stage: Too late

**Detection:**
- Characters who enter Miasma risk infection
- Game warns: "⚠️ EXPOSED TO MIASMA"
- 10% chance infection per hour in Miasma
- Symptoms appear over 14 days

### Infection Stages

| Stage | Days | Symptoms | Cureable? |
|-------|------|----------|------------|
| Exposed | 0-3 | None | 100% |
| Early | 4-7 | Headaches, confusion | 90% |
| Moderate | 8-11 | Mood swings, aggression | 70% |
| Late | 12-14 | Loss of control | 30% |
| Full | 15+ | Zombie/Mutation | 0% |

### Curing Process

```
DAY 1-4: SEDATION
- Character sedated
- Immune system suppressed
- Parasite confused
- No symptoms progression

DAY 5-8: CHEMICAL BLOCKER  
- Special herbs/chemicals administered
- Blocks parasite's neural connection
- Host starts regaining control

DAY 9-12: PARASITE DEATH
- Parasite starves slowly
- Releases toxins SLOWLY
- Host body filters toxins
- Monitoring critical

DAY 13-14: RECOVERY
- Parasite fully dead
- Host recovers
- Full health restored
- Immune to future infection!
```

### Success/Failure

**Success (90%):**
- Character fully cured
- +10 permanent health
- Immune to Miasma infection
- May have slight scar: "Survivor"

**Failure (10%):**
- Character dies
- Must be cremated immediately!
- (Body in Miasma = new plant!)

## CLEARING THE MIASMA

### After Curing Enough People

**Requirement:** 
- At least 10 cured survivors
- OR 5 cured combat-ready characters

**Then you can research:**

### Miasma Herbicide (Tech 5)

**Research Requirements:**
- Laboratory
- Science skill 50+
- Chemistry skill 40+
- Research time: 30 days

**Materials:**
- Herbs (reishi, cordyceps): 100
- Chemicals: 50
- Equipment: 20

### Application Methods

| Method | Coverage | Danger |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Sprayer | 1 tile/day | Low |
| Explosives | 5 tiles | High (toxic) |
| Aerial (Drone) | 10 tiles | Medium |

### Clearing Process

```
Team approaches Miasma edge:
- Characters with immunity (cured) enter
- Apply herbicide to plants
- Plants die within hours
- Area becomes clear!

BUT:
- Parasites in area panic
- Release toxins rapidly
- Must wear protective gear
- Only immune characters safe
```
