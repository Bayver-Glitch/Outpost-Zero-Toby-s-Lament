## CHARACTER COMMENTARY SYSTEM

### Small Talk (Ambient)

**Triggers:**
- Player is observing character
- Character is idle for >30 seconds
- During travel/loading screens
- After certain events

**Types of Comments:**

#### Work Comments
```
[Marcus is farming]
Marcus: "These tomatoes are coming in nice this year."
Marcus: "Whoever thought farming would be my life, huh?"

[Maria is cooking]
Maria: "This stew could use some more salt..."
Maria: "Mm, the smell reminds me of my grandmother's kitchen."
```

#### Idle Comments
```
[Wei is resting]
Wei: "I wonder if my mother is still in Shanghai..."
Wei: "The stars are brighter here than in the city."

[Kenji is at the bar]
Kenji: "Another night, another apocalypse."
Kenji: "Anyone up for a game of cards?"
```

### Event-Based Commentary

**When someone leaves:**
```
[Terry Johnson left the camp]

Marcus: "Did you hear about Terry? Just up and left."

Maria: "Yeah, sum bitch owed me two bottles of moonshine..."

Marcus: "He said he was going to find his family. 
         I hope he made it."

Wei: "The roads are dangerous. I hope he's okay."
```

**When someone dies:**
```
[Marcus died in combat]

Phyllis: "Oh no... Marcus was a good man."

Maria: "I'll... I'll make something for his family.
        What do families even do now?"

Carlos: "We should build something. A memorial.
         He deserves that."
```

**When baby is born:**
```
[Lin Mei gave birth to twins!]

Maria: "TWINS?! Let me see! Let me see!"

Kenji: "Congratulations! This is... this is good.
        We needed some good news."

Carlos: "Two mouths to feed, but also two more 
        hands to work. That's the way it goes."
```

**After raid:**
```
[Raiders repelled]

Wei: "That was too close. We need better walls."

Marcus: "At least we got some of their supplies.
         Not all bad."

Maria: "Is everyone okay? Anyone hurt?"
```

### Character Relationships

**Commentary varies by relationship:**

```
[High relationship - best friends]

Kenji: "Hey Wei! Want to go scavenge later?"

Wei: "Sure. Stay close to me out there."

[Low relationship]

Kenji: "Ugh, that guy. Never talks."

Wei: "Some people are just... difficult."
```

### Commentary Rules

1. **Not constant:** Max 1 comment per 30 seconds
2. **Context matters:** Comments relate to current situation
3. **Personality-based:** Quiet characters speak less
4. **Relationship-based:** Friends chat, enemies don't
5. **Emotional state:** Unhappy characters complain more
