# OUTPOST ZERO - Children, Skills & Food System

## CHILDREN SYSTEM

### Pregnancy

**Requirements:**
1. Two adult characters (18+) of opposite sex (or configurable)
2. Compatibility score 75+ (romance level)
3. Both willing (relationship check)
4. Adequate resources: Food 50+ in storage
5. Adequate housing: Comfort 25+ in camp
6. Not already pregnant (one pregnancy per female at a time)

**Chance:**
- Base chance: 10% per month of high relationship
- +5% if both "Want Children" trait
- -10% if "Infertile" trait
- Higher tech level = better medical = higher chance

### Pregnancy Stages

| Stage | In-Game Time | Real Time |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Conception | Day 0 | Immediate |
| Early Pregnancy | Days 1-15 | Task available |
| Late Pregnancy | Days 15-30 | Can't do strenuous work |
| Birth | Day 30 | Event triggered |

**Mother Effects:**
- Work efficiency: -50% late pregnancy
- Mood: Fluctuates
- Needs: Higher food/water intake
- Medical: Risk of complications without medicine

### Birth

**Trigger:** Day 30
**Event:** Random birth or assisted birth (if Medic available)

**Outcomes:**
- Single child (70% chance)
- Twins (25% chance)
- triplets (5% chance) - very rare

**Complications:**
- Without medicine: 10% chance of issues
- With doctor: 2% chance

### Child Stages

| Stage | Age | In-Game Time | Can Work | Can Learn |
|-------|-----|--------------|----------|-----------|
| Newborn | 0-7 days | 7 days | No | No |
| Small Child | 8-30 days | 23 days | No | Yes (school) |
| Teenager | 30-60 days | 30 days | Yes (light duty) | Yes (school) |
| Adult | 60+ days | - | Yes | Yes |

### Childcare

**Newborn Care (First 7 days):**
- Requires mother (or designated caretaker)
- Mother cannot work during this time
- Feeding: 2x normal food consumption
- If no mother available: -20% survival chance

**Small Child (7-30 days):**
- Can attend school
- Can do light chores (+1-3 materials/food per day)
- Needs: 1.5x food

**Teenager (30-60 days):**
- Can take job assignments (light duty)
- Can attend school
- Needs: Normal adult food

## ADULT SKILL TRAINING

### Training System

Adults can learn new skills through:

1. **Books/Magazines**
   - Can teach themselves if Education level sufficient
   - Takes time (hours per skill point)

2. **Teaching**
   - Character with Teaching 30+ can train others
   - Student needs Education 2+ (basic reading)
   - Training takes 1 hour per skill point

3. **Practice**
   - Work = XP gain
   - Slower than books/teaching

### Training Time (Hours)

| Current Skill | To Level 10 | To Level 20 | To Level 30 |
|--------------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| Books | 50 hours | 150 hours | 300 hours |
| Teaching | 35 hours | 100 hours | 200 hours |
| Practice | 70 hours | 200 hours | 400 hours |

## KITCHEN & COOKING SYSTEM

### Kitchen Requirements

| Building | Tech Level | Cooks | Storage |
|----------|------------|-------|---------|
| None | - | No | Storage box |
| Basic Kitchen | 2 | 1 | 50 food |
| Kitchen II | 3 | 2 | 100 food |
| Professional Kitchen | 4 | 3 | 200 food |
| Restaurant | 5 | 5 | 300 food |

### Cooking Process

**Without Kitchen:**
- Characters grab random food from storage
- Takes 5 minutes
- No bonuses

**With Kitchen:**
1. Cook assigned (must have Cooking skill 20+)
2. Morning: Cook prepares breakfast
3. Midday: Cook prepares lunch
4. Evening: Cook prepares dinner
5. Characters line up to eat

### Cook Efficiency

| Cooking Skill | Meals/Day | Quality Bonus |
|--------------|-----------|---------------|
| 1-20 | 1 | +0% |
| 21-40 | 2 | +10% |
| 41-60 | 3 | +25% |
| 61-80 | 4 | +50% |
| 81-100 | 5 | +75% |

### Meal Times

| Meal | Time | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| Breakfast | Morning | +10% work efficiency for morning shift |
| Lunch | Midday | +5% work efficiency for afternoon |
| Dinner | Evening | +15% overnight recovery |

## EXAMPLE: A DAY OF MEALS

**Day 15 - Settlement Level 2, Kitchen**

**Morning:**
- Cook: Marcus (Cooking skill 35)
- Kitchen has: 20 lbs meat, 15 lbs vegetables, 10 lbs bread
- Prepares: Hearty Breakfast (meat + bread)
- Result: +15% morning work efficiency

**Midday:**
- No kitchen bonus (lunch not prepared)
- Characters eat leftover bread: +10 stamina

**Evening:**
- Cook: Marcus prepares Hearty Stew
- Uses: 10 lbs meat, 8 lbs vegetables
- Result: +20 stamina, +5 health, +10 contentment

**Nutrition Summary:**
- All characters at 90%+ fullness
- Morning bonus: 3 characters completed tasks faster
- Evening bonus: +15 overnight recovery
- Net: +35 contentment for the day
