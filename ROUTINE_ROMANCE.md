## ROMANCE SYSTEM

### Attraction Factors

#### What Creates Attraction

| Factor | Examples | Weight |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Physical | Appearance, health | Low |
| Personality | Similar, opposite | Medium |
| Skills | Admires their ability | Medium |
| Kindness | Helps them | High |
| Proximity | Works nearby | High |
| Status | Job, influence | Low |
| Mysterious | Unknown about them | Medium |

### Attraction Calculation

```
Attraction Score =

+ Proximity (worked nearby): +20
+ Kindness (helped): +15
+ Personality (similar): +10
+ Skills (admire): +10
+ Status (respected): +5
- Physical (not their type): -5
= TOTAL: 50/100 (Crush!)
```

---

### Types of Attraction

#### Common Interests Attract

```
Paul + Wei:
- Both introverted
- Both like quiet work
- Both miss home (China)
- Similar personality = HIGH attraction
```

#### Opposites Attract

```
Maria (Extrovert) + Carlos (Introvert):
- She's drawn to his quiet strength
- He's drawn to her warmth
- DIFFERENT personality = HIGH attraction
```
