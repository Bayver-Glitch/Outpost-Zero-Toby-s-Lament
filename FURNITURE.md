## SKILL TRAINING

### Learning Professions

| Skill | Where to Learn | Books |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| Carpentry | Master carpenter | "Woodworking Basics" |
| Masonry | Master mason | "Stone Masonry Guide" |
| Electrical | Tech 4+, books | "Electrical Basics" |

### Training Requirements

```
LEARNING CARPENTRY:

Option 1: Books
- Find "Woodworking Basics"
- Self-teach over time
- Takes: 40 hours per level

Option 2: Teacher
- Find carpenter with Carpentry 30+
- Learn from them
- Takes: 25 hours per level

Option 3: Practice
- Do carpentry work
- Slowest but free
- Takes: 60 hours per level
```
