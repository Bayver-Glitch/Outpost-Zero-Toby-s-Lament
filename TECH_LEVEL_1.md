# OUTPOST ZERO - Tech Level 1: The Scrap Era

## GOAL: Playable Scrap Era First

**Focus:** Get ALL Tech Level 1 buildings and systems working.
- No fancy stuff
- Just survival
- If graphics were added, this would be playable
- Later: Add Tech 2-5 with new buildings

---

## TECH LEVEL 1: SCAVENGER

### Philosophy
- Use what's available
- Nothing is built, everything is salvaged
- Temporary, rough shelters
- Focus on survival

## GAMEPLAY: SCRAP ERA

### A Typical Day

```
Morning:
- Everyone uses outdoor shower (if built)
- Eat cold breakfast from storage
- Assign jobs

Day:
- Scavengers go out
- Hunters hunt
- Gatherers gather
- Guard watches walls

Evening:
- Cook over fire pit (if kitchen not built)
- Story circle around fire
- Sleep in quarters

Night:
- Guard rotates
- Everyone sleeps

Resources:
- Food: Always scarce
- Water: Rain dependent
- Materials: Always need more
- Medicine: Critical
```
